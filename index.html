How to play this game?

This game is quite simple, at the top the color that you must guess will appear in RGB format, at the bottom you will have 6 boxes with different colors, if you select the color that agrees with the RGB notation you win 1 point.

If you make a mistake when choosing the color, don't worry! You just lose all your points!

If you doubt which color to choose, you can use the "hint" button and it will reveal the correct color for a moment, in exchange for 1 point.

If the color gamut is very similar, or you don't think you can guess correctly, you can reset the gamut by pressing the "reset" button and this will cause the colors to change. But be careful, you can only do it once each time you earn a point. Choosing this option does not take away points.

Finally, if what you like are challenges, you can increase the difficulty in the buttons with the levels (quite obvious, right?). Changing the difficulty removes all the points earned, and in the most difficult difficulty, you will have a time limit of 8 seconds to answer, if you do not guess the color in that time, you will lose all your points!

That's it, how many points in a row will you be able to score? Try it!
